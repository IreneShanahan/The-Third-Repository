Your target blood glucose range

Know your target blood glucose range. Talk to your healthcare provider about your personal glucose goals for pre- and post-meal readings. To get an idea of the range for which you should aim, see below: Time of checking Usual goal for Most people Before meals 70–130 Two hours after meals less than 180 Bedtime 90–150 A1C less than 7% *expect a 30–50 point rise from premeal glucose levels 2 hours after a meal.

Food Diary Tips

1. Keep your food diary on your night stand and get into the habit of checking every night that you have filled in your diary for the day. 2. If you are in company or out on a night out you can take a photo of your meal and fill in your diary later. 3. Use everynote on your smartphone to make to make notes for your food diary. 4. If you have had a bad day or week. Write it off and start again.
